About US

Welcome to RK Jewels, Visnagar, your premier destination for exquisite jewellery. Our journey began on 28 July 2001, when our esteemed founder, Honorable Mr Rajeshbhai Kanajibhai Patel, inaugurated our showroom. Since then, we have been committed to providing our customers with the finest jewellery, exceptional service, and a shopping experience like no other.
On 22 August 2021, we proudly reopened our newly renovated showroom, once again inaugurated by Honorable Mr Rajeshbhai Kanajibhai Patel. This grand event marked a new chapter in our story, reaffirming our dedication to excellence and our passion for jewellery.
At RK Jewels, we offer a diverse collection of beautifully crafted pieces that cater to every taste and occasion. Our range includes stunning gold and silver jewellery, contemporary designs, and timeless classics, all created with meticulous attention to detail.
Our mission is to provide jewellery that not only adorns but also tells a story. Each piece in our collection is a testament to our commitment to quality, craftsmanship, and customer satisfaction. We invite you to visit our showroom and experience the elegance and beauty that define RK Jewels, Visnagar.

Thank you for being a part of our journey.

Our Services at RK jewellers, Visnagar

At RK Jewels, we offer a range of services to ensure that your jewellery experience is as unique and special as the pieces you choose.

Custom Jewellery Design

Bring your vision to life with our bespoke jewellery design service. Our skilled designers will work with you to create a one-of-a-kind piece that reflects your personal style and story.

Jewellery Repair and Restoration

Our expert craftsmen are here to help restore your treasured pieces to their original glory. From simple repairs to complex restorations, we handle each piece with the utmost care and precision.

Jewellery Cleaning and Maintenance

Keep your jewellery looking its best with our professional cleaning and maintenance services. Regular upkeep ensures that your pieces continue to shine and sparkle for years to come.

Appraisal Services

Whether you need an appraisal for insurance purposes or simply want to know the value of your jewellery, our certified gemmologists provide accurate and reliable appraisal services.

Bridal and Special Occasion Jewellery

Make your special moments even more memorable with our stunning bridal and special occasion jewellery. From engagement rings to wedding sets and elegant pieces for other milestones, we have something for every celebration.
